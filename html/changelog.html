19 January 2021

Rohesie updated:

  • Regular and material floor tile recipes no longer have the same name. You can seal your breaches with less chance of producing the wrong stack.

Thalpy updated:

  • Fixes reacted wine instantly turning into grappa by setting the grappa reaction's enzyme volume requirement to 10u instead of 5u

18 January 2021

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • Civilian Bounties now show the fulfillment value, instead of the value of the cube total. The UI is not lying anymore!

Nari Harimoto updated:

  • fixed a random space tile in science

Thunder12345 updated:

  • In-spect scanner now takes a few seconds to produce a report.

Timberpoes updated:

  • Exosuit console EMP logging now properly outputs the mech's occupants to chat and logs.
  • Mechs no longer runtime when exosuit consoles EMP them and will now properly play sounds and give notifications to their occupants when this happens.

c420-o updated:

  • fixed a single typo

nightred updated:

  • Able to secure grinders/cell chargers to tables.

park66665, Ghilker, Rohesie updated:

  • fixed atmos component Destroy() a little bit.

stylemistake updated:

  • Fixed broken UI in ExosuitFabricator interface.

17 January 2021

Cartographer-D updated:

  • all large guns except traitor objectives and those specifically made to be concealed are now bulky.

Gamer025 updated:

  • Update_Parallax no longer runtimes when observing a player without client

LemonInTheDark updated:

  • Fire alarms that have been triggered by atmos issues will now auto resolve when those issues go away.
  • Fire alarms that are being triggered by atmos issues have a blinking overlay around their frame, keep an eye out for the problem children.

TemporalOroboros updated:

  • Fixed showers overexposing things they washed
  • Added the ability to toggle showers' water regeneration with a multitool.
  • Bespoke materials exist
  • You can tell who's meat was used to make that chair

Time-Green updated:

  • Some assistants have started tracking everyones maintenance pill consumption. Will you hold the record?

Tlaltecuhtli updated:

  • Long-To-Short-Range-Bluespace-Transceiver timers changed from T1: 90s -> 40s T2: 70s -> 30s T3: 60s -> 20s T4: 60s -> 10s

cacogen updated:

  • After some setbacks in the employee navigation sector, Nanotrasen in its infinite wisdom have given the wayfinding pinpointer dispenser a personality lift in the hope of selling more pinpointers
  • Based on overwhelming demand, wayfinding pinpointers are now free. You can still refund them at the dispenser though
  • Moves some of the pointing code from the mob pointed() verb to an atom pointat() proc so everything can be made to point fingers at everything else and the animation is consistent. Now the wayfinding dispenser points like you do!

interestingusernam3 updated:

  • The holiday observed on every 4th of July is now called "US Independence Day" instead of just "Independence Day".

tralezab updated:

  • Food Cart has been completely remade to work as a mobile kitchen
